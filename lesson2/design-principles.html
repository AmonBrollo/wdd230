Design Principles

Amon Brollo

Alignment

Yuool

yuool.com.br

Looking at the page first glace the user can see a pleasing alignment of the companies product one by one.

Contrast

Target

vans.com.br

Vans does a really nice job of contrasting their shoes' colors with their background. If the pair of shoes does not contrast well with the design then they have an alternitive background color to those shoes.

Proximity

PayPal

adidas.com.br

Adidas does a great job of not only aligning their product's picture, but also the text under it. The title and subtitle are in a proximity to optimize readability and clearness.
